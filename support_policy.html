If you need help with the AKS Connect app, contact us and we’ll respond as soon as possible.

Contact

Email: webadmin@alphakappasigma-alumni.org

Tip: Include your device model + iOS version and what you were trying to do when the issue happened.

Common questions

  • I can’t sign in — Try switching networks, then close and reopen the app. If it persists, email support.
  • Notifications — Manage notification permissions in iOS Settings → Notifications → AKS Connect.
  • Event or announcement content looks wrong — Email support with a screenshot and the item title.

Account deletion

If you want your account deleted, you can initiate deletion within the app (if available) or email support. Deletion removes your authentication record and associated app data we control, unless retention is required by law.
