AKS Connect Support

Alpha Kappa Sigma Alumni Inc • Updated: January 8, 2026

If you need help with the AKS Connect app, contact us and we’ll respond within 1–2 business days.

Contact

Email: webadmin@alphakappasigma-alumni.org

Tip: Include your device model, iOS version, and what you were trying to do when the issue happened.

Please avoid sending sensitive personal information by email.

Common questions

  • I can’t sign in — Try switching networks, then close and reopen the app. If it persists, email support.
  • Verification status — If you’re not verified yet or think your status is incorrect, email support from the address you used to sign in.
  • Notifications — Manage notification permissions in iOS Settings → Notifications → AKS Connect.
  • Event or announcement content looks wrong — Email support with a screenshot and the item title.

Account deletion

To request account deletion, email webadmin@alphakappasigma-alumni.org from the address you used to sign in.

Deletion removes your authentication record and associated app data we control, unless retention is required by law.
