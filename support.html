Privacy Policy

AKS Connect • Last updated: January 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Alpha Kappa Sigma Alumni Inc (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use the AKS Connect mobile application (“App”).

Information we collect

  • Account info: When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive basic profile information such as your name and email address (depending on what you choose to share).
  • App content: Information you choose to view or submit in the App (for example, interacting with announcements or events), if applicable.
  • Diagnostics: We may collect crash/diagnostic data to improve reliability.

How we use information

  • To authenticate you and provide App features.
  • To operate and maintain the App, including troubleshooting and improving performance.
  • To respond to support requests.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers needed to operate the App (for example, authentication providers such as Apple/Google, and backend infrastructure providers if used).

Data retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., security).

Account deletion

You can request deletion from within the App (if available) or by contacting us at webadmin@alphakappasigma-alumni.org. Deleting your account removes your account from our records and associated App data that we control, unless we are legally required to retain it.

Children

The App is intended for alumni and members. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions? Email us at webadmin@alphakappasigma-alumni.org.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
